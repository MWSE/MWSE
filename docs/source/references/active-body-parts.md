# Active Body Parts

!!! tip
	These values are available in Lua by their index in the `tes3.activeBodyPart` table. For example, `tes3.activeBodyPart.hair` has a value of `1`.

------------- | -----
head          | `0`
hair          | `1`
neck          | `2`
chest         | `3`
groin         | `4`
skirt         | `5`
rightHand     | `6`
leftHand      | `7`
rightWrist    | `8`
leftWrist     | `9`
shield        | `10`
rightForearm  | `11`
leftForearm   | `12`
rightUpperArm | `13`
leftUpperArm  | `14`
rightFoot     | `15`
leftFoot      | `16`
rightAnkle    | `17`

weapon        | `25`
tail          | `26`
